If you haven't logged in yet, you will be presented with a login page. If you're creating a new account, you'll need to follow the steps to create the account and then let me know so I can assign you to your team. Once that's done, the next visit to the Portal page will present the correct information.

The first box on the Portal page is a button to provide access to the Status Management Menu. You're free to read the information provided below it before entering or just go on in and begin entering data.

At the upper right corner of every page is a Home / Logout (your name) pair of links. Home returns you to the Status Management Menu and of course, Logout logs you out of the system.
