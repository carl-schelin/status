If you're managing your time, it will be displayed here.

There is a bit more you can do here.

The title bar has a pair of less than and greater than characters along with a single set. Clicking on the double less than character link (<<) will subtract one from the starting week giving you one additional week view. Clicking on the double greater than character link (>>) will add one to the ending week again providing an additional weeks data to view. This might be interesting if you want to see how many hours you worked over a period of time.

Clicking on the single less than or greater than character links (<>) will move both the start and end week values by one week.
